🍁 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

What we collect

When you sign this petition we collect:

  • Your selected province or territory
  • A one-way cryptographic hash (SHA-256) derived from your IP address and browser information, used solely to prevent duplicate signatures. This hash cannot be reversed to identify you.
  • The timestamp of your submission

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any government-issued ID number.

How we use it

Data is used only to display the live signature count and province breakdown, and to enforce one-signature-per-device. It is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing.

Storage

Data is stored on Supabase (Postgres) servers hosted in Canada or the United States. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified.

Your rights (PIPEDA)

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act you have the right to access information held about you and to request its deletion. Because we store only an irreversible hash, we cannot link stored data back to you without your co-operation in providing your original IP address.

Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact the petition operator via the contact information listed on the main page.
